Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Electa collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Electa collects information that you provide directly to us, such as when you save votes or interact with our website. This may include:

  • Vote preferences saved in your browser's local storage
  • Usage data and analytics information
  • Information you provide when contacting us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Remember your saved votes and preferences
  • Understand how users interact with our website
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support

3. Local Storage

Electa does not collect or store personal political opinions on our servers. Any preferences saved (such as favourited candidates or saved votes) are stored locally on the user's device using browser local storage and are not transmitted to Electa. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Electa uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and provide relevant content. We use three types of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function properly. These cookies enable basic features like page navigation and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. These cookies require your consent.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to show you relevant ads based on your interests. These cookies require your consent and are provided by third-party services like Google AdSense.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the link in our footer or by using your browser settings.

4.1 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies or manage your preferences.
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete cookies. The methods for doing so vary from browser to browser.
  • Third-Party Tools: You can use third-party tools to manage tracking cookies across multiple websites.

Please note that blocking or deleting cookies may impact your experience on our website and limit certain features.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • External Links: Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services or websites you interact with.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.

11. GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

We process your personal data based on your consent (for cookies and tracking) or our legitimate interests (for providing our services). You can withdraw your consent at any time through our cookie settings.
