Our Vision

Electa helps Australians understand electorates, candidates, and the issues shaping each campaign. We gather context on local issues, profile every candidate, and explain the policies that matter in plain language.

Our goal is to increase voter knowledge, clarity, and confidence. By demystifying policy positions and offering clear comparisons, Electa empowers voters across Australia - whether they are casting a ballot for the first time or refining decades of civic participation.
